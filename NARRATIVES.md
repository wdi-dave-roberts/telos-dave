## Breaking the Fighting Cycle

Our relationship has a pattern of intense fighting over feelings, but we're working to break that cycle by acknowledging our past mistakes and taking steps towards growth. I'm starting to see my part in this dynamic more clearly.

This isn't a resolution story yet. It's a recognizing story — both of us seeing what we do, maybe for the first time at the same time.

## The Longing to Be Seen

I carry enormous awareness — of other people's pain, patterns, complexity. I hold space, translate emotions, connect dots, often without anyone realizing the effort. That capacity is real and it costs something.

Underneath it is a longing: to be seen without having to explain myself. To be understood without performing. To have someone recognize not just what I do, but who I am underneath it. The same depth I bring to others — I want it returned, at least sometimes, at least by the person closest to me.

This connects directly to the feelings dynamic with Tonia. The pattern where my emotions get explained away isn't just frustrating — it's the exact opposite of what I'm longing for. I'm asking to be seen, and the response I get is that what I'm showing isn't real.

**Status:** Named. Sitting with it.
