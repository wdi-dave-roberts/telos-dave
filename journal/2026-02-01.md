## Prompt: What changed about security

We were a family of means by the time we left. I was hugely successful in my career but I found myself feeling so unfulfilled. Nothing felt like it meant anything. I didn't know what to do with these feelings so I began searching for, what some would consider, radical approaches. I joined the mushroom church of Austin and hired a "priestess" to administer "the spirit" — psilocybin. I prepared myself.

Tonia has a dream to own our own business, an Inn, but I couldn't get past my fear of poverty. I was raised in poverty so I've always hyper protected our means. I invested conservatively, I lived in a house far beneath my means, I never overspent on things like cars or boats. But investing in myself, I couldn't go there. I didn't believe in myself enough. I felt like I would fail.

The spirit took that fear away and opened a door to being willing to consider this radical change. My engineer brain didn't fall asleep — it woke up and applied itself to discovering if this Inn idea was a fantasy, fun to talk about, but not feasible... to realizing, it was very feasible. Without the fear, I was able to take the ultimate step and do it.
