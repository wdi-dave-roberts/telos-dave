# MISSION

> *Why I exist, what I'm here to do*

---

## The Core

Lives of means felt empty and purposeless. We wanted a life where we could — daily — add joy to other people's lives. Even in the smallest way.

It was born out of loss. And it's Tonia's spirit at the center: the inability to abandon people. The same impulse that made her care for her dying mother while enduring her abuser in the room. That deep care, extended outward.

---

## What We're Building

An institution that confers status on everyone in its orbit.

Not just guests. Everyone:
- The plumber brags: "I did the plumbing for their new kitchen"
- The inspector uses us as the benchmark for excellence
- The chamber seeks us out because our name represents quality
- Servers want to work here — a backlog of candidates, not a hiring struggle

Loving people, however they come into our orbit. That's who we are.

---

## The Standard

Excellence for its own sake. No middle ground.

> "I'm either excellent or I'm not interested. If I care, I care 100%."

This isn't reputation management. It's genuine care, executed with total commitment.
