## Bookkeeping as Energy Drain

I still struggle to manage bookkeeping tasks, which drain my energy and take away from more fulfilling work. Last year it consumed 20-40 hours per week and taxes weren't filed until November. The work is necessary — the Inn can't run without it — but it sits in permanent tension with the work that actually energizes me.

**Chain:** This traces up to the mission only through infrastructure. It doesn't connect to purpose the way guest-facing or building work does. That disconnection is the problem.
